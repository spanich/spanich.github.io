A Bit About Myself

I am inspired by natural beauty, fashion, make-up, dance, acting, and art. I enjoy modeling, performing, and living a healthy life style.

Growing up, I was a rhythmic gymnast, and participated in national competitions. In high school, I was part of Fantasy Dance Group. We performed at local arts and wine festivals, on the Alaska Cruise, and at other privately and publicly-held events around the SF Bay Area, and did runway modeling for Fashion Passion clothing store.

Please feel free to reach out to me, I would love to meet you!
